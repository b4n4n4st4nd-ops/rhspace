## Concept

An exploration at the intersection of data, light, and sound — translating audio features into spatial LED patterns for live performance contexts.

## Process

- Mapped frequency bands to color zones on addressable LED strips via WLED
- Built a Python bridge receiving OSC from Ableton Live for beat and amplitude events
- Tuned easing and decay curves so visuals feel musical rather than reactive noise

## Status

Work in progress — refining mapping presets and documenting the protocol for reproducible setups.
