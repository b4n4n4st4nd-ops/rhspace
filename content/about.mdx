I'm a **visualization developer** and **analytics engineer** who builds BI platforms, data pipelines, and AI-assisted workflows. I work across Tableau, Alteryx, SQL, Python, and modern web stacks — platform-agnostic, outcome-focused.

## My path

I started in reactive dashboarding: answering the questions stakeholders already knew to ask. Over time that grew into owning the full analytics stack — ingestion, modeling, visualization, and the glue code that keeps it reliable.

More recently I've been designing **AI-driven workflows**: agents that route requests, call tools, and expose their reasoning so humans stay in the loop. The through-line is the same — make complex data legible and actionable.

## What I bring

- **Visualization craft** — executive dashboards, exploratory analysis, design systems for metrics
- **Analytics engineering** — ETL, warehouse modeling, data quality gates
- **Automation** — Alteryx, Python, orchestration that scales beyond one-off scripts
- **Agent development** — tool interfaces, API proxies, demo UIs with observable logs
- **Creative technology** — LED installations, generative visuals, live performance systems

## How I work

I partner closely with stakeholders, document decisions, and ship iteratively. Whether the deliverable is a Tableau workbook, a Snowflake model, or a Next.js demo — I care about maintainability and the person on the other side of the screen.
