## Situation

A global retail organization needed a single source of truth for executive KPIs. Regional teams maintained separate spreadsheets and Tableau workbooks with inconsistent definitions.

## Task

Design and deliver a governed executive dashboard with standardized metrics, drill-down capability, and automated refresh from the warehouse.

## Action

- Modeled star schema in Snowflake with documented metric definitions
- Built Tableau dashboards with parameter-driven region and product filters
- Implemented row-level security aligned to leadership access tiers
- Partnered with stakeholders on UX reviews and iteration cycles

## Result

Weekly reporting preparation dropped from ~8 hours to ~3 hours. Executive adoption reached 40+ active users within the first quarter of launch.
