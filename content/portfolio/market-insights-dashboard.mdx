## Situation

Market-facing teams often need to combine internal performance, external demand signals, segment behavior, and competitive movement before they can decide where to focus growth efforts.

## Task

Create a clean-room market insights dashboard that turns scattered market indicators into a guided reporting experience for strategic planning and business reviews.

## Planned Action

- Generate synthetic market, customer segment, channel, and competitor benchmark data
- Build trend views for demand, share movement, price position, and regional opportunity
- Add segmentation cuts that compare growth, retention, profitability, and engagement signals
- Include insight callouts that separate structural trends from short-term noise

## Planned Demo Outcome

The finished demo will show how market intelligence reporting can help leaders prioritize opportunities, pressure-test strategy, and align teams around evidence-based growth narratives.
