## Situation

Optimization and recommendation systems only create value when stakeholders can understand whether the model is improving decisions, where recommendations are being accepted, and where human overrides reveal better business rules.

## Task

Build a clean-room reporting demo that evaluates an optimization and recommendation engine across model performance, business impact, adoption, and exception handling.

## Planned Action

- Create synthetic recommendation output with confidence scores, expected lift, accepted actions, rejected actions, and override reasons
- Model performance views for precision, recall, coverage, service levels, and revenue or margin impact
- Design operational drilldowns that show recommendation quality by region, category, segment, and time period
- Add an exception review workflow for low-confidence recommendations, stale inputs, and repeated overrides

## Planned Demo Outcome

The finished demo will show how analytics teams can make recommendation engines transparent enough for executives, operators, and product owners to trust and improve over time.
