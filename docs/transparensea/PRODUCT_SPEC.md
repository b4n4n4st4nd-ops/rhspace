# Transparensea — Product Specification

**Product:** Transparensea  
**Descriptor:** Model Transparency, Adoption & Impact  
**Demonstration brand:** A.Typical  
**Surface:** Lab Streamlit experience (`streamlit/model-ops-dashboard`)

## Purpose

Transparensea connects production model inputs, outputs, business adoption, and downstream outcomes so model product owners and business stakeholders can close the gap between building a model and realizing business value.

## Demonstration use case

A.Typical runs a monthly marketing treatment-selection / uplift model that recommends:

- New Season Edit
- Performance Collection
- Suppression (no communication)

plus related execution concepts (Member Access, Re-Engagement, Follow-Up).

## Required experiences

1. Model Overview  
2. Input Transparency  
3. Recommendations  
4. Adoption  
5. Conversion & Revenue  
6. Exceptions & Outliers  
7. Insights & Actions  
8. Improvement History  
9. Model Details  

## Non-goals (deferred)

Customer configuration wizard, live warehouse credentials, SSO, multi-tenant hosting, Jira/Slack sync, automated retraining, billing.

## Integrity rules

- No Nike or proprietary employer data
- A.Typical is fictional
- Simulated financials are labeled as simulated
- Automated insights are deterministic analytics templates (not LLM-generated)
- Portfolio predictive-model case study remains separate from this Lab product demo
